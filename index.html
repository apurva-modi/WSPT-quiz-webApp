About the Exam

Hello Monarch and welcome to the Writing Success Placement Tool (WSPT)!

Welcome to the Writing Success Placement Tool! The WSPT measures studentsâ€™ writing and reading abilities and considers their previous learning experiences to determine which ODU English composition course sequence is the best fit for them. The WSPT uses a combination of high school GPA, performance on writing, reading, and grammar diagnostics, and self-assessment to make this determination. Anyone entering Old Dominion University without credit for ENGL 110 English Composition should take the WSPT. Students will receive their score and directions for registration via email within 48 hours of completion.

You are encouraged to take the WSPT when you can devote a couple of hours to reading and responding to questions, drafting and revising your essay, and answering the survey questions. Please note that it is in your best interest to answer all questions as honestly as possible to ensure that you are accurately placed. There is no time limit for taking the test, but the test tool does not allow students to change previous responses. The WSPT should be submitted at least 3 BUSINESS DAYS (Monday through Friday) prior to the start of your orientation. If you fail to meet this deadline, your placement data may not be updated in the system, which can affect your ability to register for courses.
